Welcome to TrickTurn! In this game, you're in Halligan, you've just realized that it's 4:45PM, and you really hope to get into Dewick on the tail end of a lunch swipe so that you can get Hodgdon later tonight. Well, get a move on!

Use the arrow keys to move around. Try not to get hit by cars! Answer professors' questions as quickly as possible so that you can continue on and get to Dewick before the lunch period runs out. Lowest time wins!
