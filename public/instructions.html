Welcome to TrickTurn! You find yourself in Halligan at 4:45PM, and you hope to get to Dewick and swipe in on your lunch swipe before 5! Run through busy intersections, answer pesky professors, and get to Dewick on time for the sake of a late night Hodgdon run!

Use the arrow keys to move. When professors stop you to ask questions, answer them as quickly as possible to continue. Get to Dewick as fast as possible for a high score.
